About Me

Hey, my name is Barry. I'm an aspiring web developer living in Cary, NC and currently learning new things every day through the UNC-Chapel Hill Coding Boot Camp. Prior to this, I have done some hobbyist game development and wanted to turn my hobby into a career that's both in demand and allows for creativity.

I've always enjoyed technology, and since I can remember I've always enjoyed creating things. Circumstances finally lined up in a way to allow me to pursue a career combining the two, something that has been in the back of my head for nearly my entire adult life.

I live with my wife, Hailey, our two cats, Walter and Paul, and our dog, Dobby. We enjoy watching good movies, eating good food, and having good laughs.
